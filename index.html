Hi I'm johoice! A Fullstack developer from Germany. I'm mainly working with Java, Python, JavaScript and TypeScript. Lately I'm getting more and more into Go aswell.

Why johoice?

The ice part originated in our school library a long time ago. A classmate showed us an online browser game (OGame I believe). His username was firefighter. So naturally we all started using names like waterfigher, earthfighter and I picked icefighter. The fighter part sounded quite lame after a while so just the ice stayed.

The origin of joho is quite similar. I used the name first on a LAN party. At some point changing our names in CS 1.6 was funnier than actually playing. Someone started with the name juhu (like a yay in german) and everybody else followed with jaha, jihi, jehe and my choice was joho. After the LAN I just kept the name and didn't change it back.

As joho is sadly often already taken on various websites and services I added the ice part. So the name is a nod to the early days of my gaming experiences.
